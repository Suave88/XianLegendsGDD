Target Audience

Who We're Making This For

Primary Audience

JRPG Enthusiasts (Ages 18-35)

  • Grew up on Fire Emblem, Persona, Tales series
  • Value character relationships and emotional stories
  • Want strategic depth without punishing difficulty
  • Prefer games that respect their time (20-30 hour campaigns)
  • Appreciate lighthearted tones over grimdark

Secondary Audience

Cozy Game Players

  • Stardew Valley, Animal Crossing fans
  • Want more structured gameplay with clear goals
  • Enjoy base building and customization
  • Prefer positive, uplifting experiences

Tactical RPG Fans

  • Love grid-based combat but tired of permadeath stress
  • Want emotional narratives without tragedy
  • Seek games with meaningful character interactions
